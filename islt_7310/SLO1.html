Advocacy Letter to Congressman William Lacy Clay


This letter was written as the final project for the “Foundations of Library and Information Science” completed in the Fall of 2018. After learning about the ethics of librarianship and the importance of advocacy, we were instructed to write a letter in order to persuade an individual or group into supporting an issue we found in the field. I chose to write a letter in the hopes of convincing a Congressman to help secure more funding for the National Personnel Records Center in orde to purchase the necessary technology to help modernize and improve our services to US veterans and their families.
