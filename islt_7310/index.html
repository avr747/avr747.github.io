Ashney Randle E-Portfolio

Welcome! I am current Masters of Library and Information Science graduate student at the University of Missouri, and my goal is to one day obtain a position where I get to manage and improve internal processes of libraries and archives.

When I entered the MLIS program in 2018, my goal was to learn more about the LIS profession. I came into the program with a lot of technical experience, but little knowledge of how the field worked. I feel that lacking a sense of what being an LIS professional is hinders my growth. Upon graduation in May 2021, I will be leaving the MLIS profession with the knowledge of what the core ethics of librarianship are, and the role I play as an LIS professional. With this portfolio, it is my hope that I am able to demonstrate two things. The first, is that you can see my what my interests are and my growth as a professional. Second, is to highlight the work I have done in the MLIS program. The work selected is what I feel demonstrates my mastery of the four student learning outcomes of the program.
